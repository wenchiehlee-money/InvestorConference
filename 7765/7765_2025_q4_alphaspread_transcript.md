CHT Security Co Ltd
Investor Relations

CHT Security Co., Ltd. provides telco-centric network security and cyber security services. The company is headquartered in Taipei City, Taipei and currently employs 367 full-time employees. The company went IPO on 2024-08-20. The firm integrates research and development, practice, and technical capabilities to provide information security products and services including pre-event detection, in-event monitoring and response, and post-incident forensic recovery, meeting the information security needs of governments and enterprises in one stop. Services include online information security protection services, professional information security services, information security consulting, information security management platform planning and construction, identity recognition products and applications, and overall solutions. Information security protection products include SecuTex, Fortinet Fortigate, Palo Alto Network NGFW, CrowdStrike Endpoint Protection, IMPERVA SecureSphere, Sophos NGFW, FireEye Endpoint Security. The firm mainly conducts business in Taiwan.
